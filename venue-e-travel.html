Congress location

Rio Othon Palace

Av. Atlântica, 3264 - 5º andar

Copacabana, Rio de Janeiro

Hotel website

The conference will be held at the Rio Othon Palace Hotel, overlooking the world-famous Copacabana Beach.

Nestled on the iconic Copacabana beachfront, the Rio Othon Palace Hotel offers guests the privilege of experiencing one of Rio de Janeiro's most captivating and celebrated postcards.

Coming from Santos Dummont Airport

Possible Route:

  • Follow Av. Presidente Vargas towards Av. Atlântica.

  • Turn right onto Av. Princesa Isabel.

  • Continue onto Av. Princesa Isabel until you reach Av. Atlântica.

  • Turn left onto Av. Atlântica.

  • The Hotel Othon will be on your right.

Coming from Galeão Airport

Possible Route:

  • Take the Via Expressa Pres. João Goulart/Linha Vermelha.
